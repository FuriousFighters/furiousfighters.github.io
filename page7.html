AN INTERVIEW WITH A PRACTITIONER OF MARTIAL ARTS

ONE OF THE memberS of the bulsu karate team

One of our developers, who will become part of BulSU's karate team officially when he enters college, conducted an interview with one of his teammates named Ralph Adrian Mamansag. 

1. Why did you decide to learn martial arts, specifically karate?

The respondent replied that learning this helps him to improve his skills as he is currently studying a course belonging to BulSU's College of Sports, Exercise and Recreation (CSER). "Napansin ko dun ako nai-improve kaya gusto kong matutunan."

2. Undoubtedly, training is hard. What drives you to continue training?

His drive is his exellence through his hardwork while also being willing to accept further improvements. "Nakikita kong nag-e-excel ako kahit mahirap." For him, it is also rewarding when all of his efforts bear fruit, by means of showing off his skill and technique during competitions.
